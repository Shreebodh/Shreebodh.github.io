About me

My name is Binisha Kandel. I've been in Australia for more than three and a half years now,but I am originally from Nepal.
Some of my hobbies are listening to music, painting, and traveling, sometimes. I appreciate you taking your time to explore my Lockdown personal blog. It is a lovely blessing to be able to use colours. Colour is more of a necessity.
It gives life. I am not just talking about the paintings on the wall but everything we see in our day-to-day life. The food that we eat, the clothes that we wear, the scenic view that we observe is completely dull without colours. I feel that the world would be little less exciting and incomplete.
